In today’s busy world people are not getting enough time to make all these traditional recipes so we love to serve them and so as preserve our culture. Now we have established ourselves to making minimum 3000-4000 Puranpoli’s daily in case of personal order number increases, selling it to 7 shops as well. The number of Puranpoli lovers is increasing day by day.

We are having a record of making 18-20k Puranpoli’s during the Holi festival in just 5 days without any Branding and still, people wait in a queue to get one. We also do catering orders, tiffin services, party orders. Modak & Diwali faral.

During Diwali, we serve people with a minimum of 300kg chakli, 2000 laddos , 250kg sev etc. We are great with cuisine like Brahmin, Jain, Gujarati, Marathi, quick bites like pav bhaaji etc. People & specially Parlekars love the hygiene we maintain & the quality of taste we give them. People come all the way from Virar, Kalyan just to have the bite of our Puranpoli.

Our customers also usually send our Puranpoli’s in foreign like Singapore, UK, USA, Hongkong, etc. Or those who have shifted from India and living apart from their family as they always want to have an experience and homemade bite of scrumptious taste.
